# Mesquite Ranch

A smoky version of the American Ranch salad dressing.

- 200 ml sour cream
- 200 ml mayonaise
- 100 ml skim buttermilk
- 15g salt
- 5 g black pepper
- 5 g rosemary
- 5 g thyme
- 1-2 g mesquite smoke flavor (titrate to taste, there is no standard formulation; mine is a dry powder on maltodextrin)
